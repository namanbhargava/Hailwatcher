🏠 Protect Your Home

🚗

Vehicles

Move cars into the garage. Hail over 1" cracks windshields. DuPage County averages 4+ significant events per year.

🏠

Home Prep

Bring in furniture, close windows and skylights before storms. Consider Class 4 impact-resistant shingles.

📸

Document Damage

Photograph hail damage immediately after any storm. Timestamped photos strengthen your insurance claim. Measure stone size if safe.

⚡

Stay Inside

During active hail, avoid windows. Interior rooms on lower floors are safest. Stay away from skylights.

📱

Get Alerts

Enable weather alerts in your iPhone Settings → Notifications → Weather. NWS Wireless Emergency Alerts require no app.

🔍

Roof Check

After any hail over 1", get a professional roof inspection. Granule loss shortens shingle life even without visible cracks.
